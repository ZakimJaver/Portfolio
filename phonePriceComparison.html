Phone Price Comparison

Platform: C++ 

                    
About Phone Price Comparison

                    This is a C++ application that compares two different phone plans and finds which plan 
                    is better for most customers. All of the data (such as customer name, call duration…) 
                    is generated randomly. There are two types of plans, regular and premium. The difference 
                    between them is how the balance is calculated:

                    Computing a Regular Customer’s balance: 
                    balance = monthlyfee + percall × num_calls
                    where monthly fee is $5, a per call rate is $1. 

                    Computing a Premium Customer’s balance: 
                    balance = monthlyfee + permin × num_mins
                    where monthly fee is $30, a per minute rate is $0.05.
                    Calls are always rounded up to the next min. I.e. 1 second call is rounded to 1 minute.
